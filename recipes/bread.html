Milk Bread

Homemade milk bread recipe

Use your sour milk to make a loaf of bread that everyone will enjoy.

A simple, easy bread machine recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1⅛ cups milk
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1½ teaspoons active dry yeast

Steps

  1. Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer
  2. Select regular white bread cycle
  3. Press Start
